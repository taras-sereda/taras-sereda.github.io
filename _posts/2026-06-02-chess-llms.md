I gave each LLM $15 and set them loose in a chess tournament.

LLMs are helpful at the tasks they're explicitly trained on. There's no evidence that frontier labs are training models to play chess, so I decided to set up a tournament where two frontier models play against each other: gpt-5.5 vs claude-4.8, both with high reasoning effort.

TLDR: gpt-5.5 beats claude-4.8 in a short game of 39 moves. gpt-5.5 was also the more expensive player, spending $8 while claude spent only $5.

Every game starts with the classic opening `e2e4` — I didn't see the LLMs get any more experimental than that.
